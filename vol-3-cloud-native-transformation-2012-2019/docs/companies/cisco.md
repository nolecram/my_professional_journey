# Cisco

## Company Overview

**Cisco Systems** is the worldwide leader in IT and networking. During this period (2012–2014), Cisco was navigating one of the most significant transitions in its history: the shift from purely hardware-centric networking to Software-Defined Networking (SDN) and the convergence of compute, network, and storage.

### Company Profile

- **Industry:** Networking, Telecommunications, Cloud Infrastructure
- **Tenure Period:** 2012 – 2014
- **Context:** The era of Cisco UCS (Unified Computing System), the birth of ACI (Application Centric Infrastructure), and the integration of networking into the cloud stack.

## The Technology Shift

This chapter represents the bridge between the physical and the virtual. It was a time of learning how to abstract the network, how to automate the data center, and how to bring the reliability of hardware into the flexibility of the cloud.
