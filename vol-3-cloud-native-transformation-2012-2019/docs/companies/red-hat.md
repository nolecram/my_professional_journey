# Red Hat

## Company Overview

**Red Hat** is the world's leading provider of enterprise open source solutions. This tenure (2014–2019) coincided with the explosive growth of the open hybrid cloud, the victory of Kubernetes as the orchestration standard, and the mainstream adoption of Ansible for automation.

### Company Profile

- **Industry:** Enterprise Open Source Software
- **Tenure Period:** 2014 – 2019
- **Context:** The "Container Wars," the shift from OpenStack to OpenShift (Kubernetes), and the culture of "Upstream First."

---

## The Open Source Culture

Working at Red Hat was an immersion in a different way of thinking. It wasn't just about the code; it was about the community. The principle that "default to open" unlocks innovation faster than any proprietary lab ever could.
